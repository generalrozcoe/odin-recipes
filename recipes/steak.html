Steak

Simple steak, simple to make

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Seasoning blend
  • Oil
  • 1/4 Onion chopped (optional)
  • Stainless Steel Pan

Steps

  1. Pre- heat your stainless pan. The way to check it's ready is throw some droplets of water on the pan and see that they skip around.
  2. Lightly coat the pan in oil, preferably non seed oil.
  3. Cook down the onions with an ocassional toss.
  4. I like my steak on the rarer side so I do 2 minutes per side. If you like to ruin your food, you can let it cook more thoroughly.
  5. The most important step is once you take the steak out, let it rest. It will soak up the precious juices that it let out in the cooking process.
