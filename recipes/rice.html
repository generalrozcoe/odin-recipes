Rice

Rice is nice and twice as nice is rice

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Rice
  • Salt
  • Green Onion
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash your rice thoroughly.
  2. Put the rice in your rice cooker with water.
  3. Stand a spoon in the middle of the rice, if it holds, you have a good amount of water.
  4. Add Oil, preferably non seed oil, and Green Onion.
  5. Turn your rice cooker on
  6. Rice cookers are automatic so it will automatically turn off when its done but to prevent the rice from drying out you can take the pot off of the heating element.
