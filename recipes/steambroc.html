Its Brocolli

Ingredients

  • Steamer
  • Brocolli
  • Soft Butter
  • Garlic and other seasonings

Steps

  1. Steam your brocolli until its your preference of tenderness. I like mine with a little crunch.
  2. Mix your garlic and other seasonings with the butter.
  3. Stir pads of the seasoned butter into the brocolli.
